A Vision ...

Troubled adolescents - are ones in most need for help and mentorship. They should be cared during their tough times and shown the right direction. When they cared they eventually become successful adults. The Vision Ministry provides mentoring service for struggling, at-risk kids as well as creates job opportunities for them. The idea not only to help by providing a job, but to teach youth how to create job opportunities by themselves through building small community service businesses like car washing, carpet cleaning and similar.

At that particular moment we focused on building car washing service presented at WAXXD.NET which will be eventually managed and fulfilled by kids. On that site people who wish to help in providing job opportunity for young individuals can order a car wash through our booking form.
